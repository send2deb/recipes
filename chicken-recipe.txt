Ingredients:
 - 1 Kg Chicken
 - 4 Onions
 - 2 Garlic
 - 2 Teaspoons ginger paste
 - 1 1/2 Teaspoons cumin powder
 - 1 1/2 Teaspoons coriander powder
 - 1 Teaspoon turmeric powder
 - 1/2 Chili powder
 - 2 Tablespoons Olive oil

Directions:
 1. Add oil to hot pan and add chopped onions to it
 
 2. Fry the onion and garlic until it's golden brown

 3. Add the ginger paste

 4. Add the other ingredients and stir well

 5. Cook for 30 mins until chicken is tender
